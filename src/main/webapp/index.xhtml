Welcome to AirAlliance

AirAlliance has evolved into one of the most respected travel brands around the world. We have one of the world's youngest fleet in the air, a network spanning five continents.

Customers, investors, partners, and staff — everyone expects excellence of us. And so, in our lounges, our conferences, working relationships, and in the smallest details of flight, we rise to each occasion and deliver the AirAlliance experience.

The airline operates over 370 flights daily across 44 destinations within India and also operates flights to United Kingdom, United States of America, Canada, Belgium, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman and Qatar on one of the youngest and best maintained fleets.

AirAlliance plans to extend its international operations further in North America, Europe, Africa and Asia in the coming years with the induction of wide-body aircraft into its fleet.
